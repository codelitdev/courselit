The easiest way to get started with CourseLit is to begin your **14-day free trial** on [courselit.app](https://courselit.app).

## Start your free school

1. Sign up for an account on [courselit.app](https://courselit.app).
2. Create a [school](/schools/create).
3. You are off to the races.
